GYM APP - INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
----------------------------------

1. Transfer 'GymApp.apk' to your Android phone.
2. Open the file on your phone.
3. If asked, 'Allow installation from unknown sources'.
4. Tap Install.
5. Open the 'Gym App' from your app menu.

Note: This is a debug build. It will work exactly like your website but as a native app.
